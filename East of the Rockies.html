East of the Rockies

East of the Rockies is an experimental narrative AR experience that follows the Japanese-Canadian internment. Told from the perspective of a 17-year-old girl internee, East of the Rockies is the story of a young millennial woman attempting to piece together the faded memory of her family’s past, set in an imagined version of the Slocan Japanese-Canadian internment camp—the site where her ailing grandmother lived and where her family’s lives changed forever.
