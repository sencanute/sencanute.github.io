The Ask

We were approached by an internal banking team with a concept of behaviour change and awareness of one’s financial wellbeing. Most Canadians are unaware of their finances and even more are unclear about the options they have to better their financial situations. We were tasked with creating a quick exploration of the way in which a smart banking app could detect these opportunities and go about bringing positive change in the user.

The Challenge

Reaching financial wellness takes time and effort from the user. The challenge here was really giving the user a sense that their financial situation is improving and providing an education piece around financial wellness.

‍

The Process

We created two scenarios for this exploration. For the first scenarios we wanted to depict a user setting financial goals and the mobile application providing options that would allow the user to achieve this goal. The goals were introduced to create a positive environment in which the user could reach financial wellness. In The second scenario we explored a user adjusting their goals to show that the application can be flexible to the needs of the user.

‍

‍

The Result

After creating the user flows and white boarding, we created wireframes and prototyped the experience to get it in the hands of users and potential clients.
